Who We Are

Bridging Technology & Business

Our mission is to explore emerging tech trends, cultivate leadership skills, and help members grow professionally in tech-driven industries. Open to students of all majors and experience levels.

1

Tech Topics

Each semester we dive into 3 relevant tech topics — from AI and cloud computing to cybersecurity — keeping members ahead of industry trends.

2

Professional Development

Build career-ready skills through resume workshops, mock interviews, case competitions, and hands-on seminars designed to boost your job readiness.

3

Guest Speakers & Networking

Hear from industry professionals sharing real-world insights on technology and business, then connect with peers, alumni, and employers at networking events.
