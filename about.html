About TMC

Shaping the Future of Business Through Technology

We are Indiana University's leading student organization at the intersection of technology and business, empowering the next generation of tech leaders.

Our Mission

Explore. Connect. Lead.

The Technology Management Club at Indiana University is the Kelley School of Business's leading organization for students passionate about technology and its role in shaping modern business.

Our mission is to explore emerging tech trends, cultivate leadership skills, and help members grow professionally in tech-driven industries.

TMC is open to students of all majors and experience levels who are curious about the intersection of business and technology. Our membership continues to grow each semester as we build a supportive, innovative, and collaborative community.

What We Do

TMC hosts a diverse range of activities designed to build skills, expand networks, and create lasting connections.

Guest Speaker Events

Industry professionals share insights on technology strategy, consulting, product management, and career development.

Networking Sessions

Curated opportunities to connect with peers, alumni, and employers from leading technology and consulting firms.

Interactive Workshops

Hands-on workshops covering everything from coding to business analytics to data visualizations.

Professional Growth

Dedicated resources, from resume reviews and project building workshops, to mock interviews and case prep designed to accelerate career readiness at the intersection of technology and business.

Get Involved

Student Leadership

At TMC, we believe in empowering our members to lead from day one. Hands-on leadership opportunities through our committee structure give you early experience that sets you apart.

Technology Committees

3 per semester

Present. Research. Connect.

Work on engaging informational presentations while connecting with industry professionals. Each semester features three unique tech-focused committees tackling real-world business technology topics.

  • AI in Business
  • Data Analytics
  • Product Management

Operational Committees

Finance Committee

Manage TMC's budget, financial planning, and resource allocation. Gain practical experience in organizational finance.

Outreach & Corporate Relations

Build relationships with industry partners, coordinate speakers, and create networking opportunities.

Professional Development

Design workshops, speaker series, and skill-building events that prepare members for tech consulting and management careers.

Marketing & Membership

Shape TMC's brand, manage social media, and drive engagement. Learn digital marketing and community building.

Why Lead at TMC?

As a committee director or member, you'll gain real leadership experience that consulting firms and tech companies value: managing teams, driving projects to completion, and making strategic decisions. Our committee structure provides the perfect balance of autonomy and mentorship, allowing you to lead with impact while developing skills that will serve you throughout your career.
